Experience Revenue Strategy:

  • Experiences drive 70% of operating income despite being only 36% of revenue
  • Q1 fiscal 2025: Experiences generated $3.1 billion in operating income
  • Engaging teens = sustainable long-term growth in high-margin experiences
  • Universal attracts teens with "thrill" experiences while Disney perceived as "for younger kids"

Experience Enhancement Strategy

Gamification

Expand Play Disney Parks app with teen-focused AR challenges. Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge Datapad shows proven success with interactive gameplay.

Teen Events

Reintroduce teen-focused after-hours events like "Teen Night" with DJs, exclusive ride access, and influencer appearances. Grad Nites consistently sell out.

Influencer Integration

Partner with teen-favorite TikTok/YouTube creators for park experiences. 61% of teens purchase items based on influencer recommendations.

Group Incentives

Create teen group discounts to encourage friend visits. 42% of Gen Z travelers influenced by friend recommendations.
