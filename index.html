The Experience Opportunity

Teens don't have shorter attention spans - they have better filters. Create experiences that pass through these filters by being immediately engaging.

Strategic Recommendations

Short-Form Content Strategy

Create "content molecules" for TikTok/YouTube that drive to longer experiences. Develop behind-the-scenes theme park content that converts to park visits.

Influencer & Collaboration Strategy

Partner with top teen influencers for virtual Disney events that connect to real-world experiences. Launch TikTok challenges at parks and Disney Creator Days.

Experience Enhancement Strategy

Implement AR park experiences, teen-focused events, and digital-physical integration like the enhanced Play Disney Parks app with exclusive teen content.

Digital to Physical Conversion Path

  • Create "FOMO" through influencer park/cruise visits
  • Develop teen-specific Disney Cruise excursions
  • Launch teen-only special events at parks
  • Implement gamified park experiences through Play Disney Parks app
